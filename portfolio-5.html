An AI-powered advertising system that detects viewer demographics in real-time and serves tailored advertisements based on gender and age classification. Integrated QR code scanning allows viewers to access additional product details, promotions, and personalized content with a single scan.

System Architecture

Real-time Detection

Live video feed processed through YOLO AI for gender and age classification with minimal latency.

Smart Ad Selection

Automatically selects and displays advertisements matching detected audience demographics.

Interactive QR System

Viewers scan QR codes to unlock promotions, detailed product info, and personalized offers.

Marketing Efficiency

Increases engagement and conversion by delivering highly relevant content to each viewer.

How It Works

  • Camera captures real-time video of individuals approaching the display.
  • YOLO AI analyzes facial features to classify gender and age groups.
  • Ad system queries database and selects best-matching advertisements.
  • Relevant ads display while QR codes enable interactive engagement.
  • System logs demographics for analytics and future optimization.
