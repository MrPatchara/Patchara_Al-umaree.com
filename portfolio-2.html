Speaker NSTDA Conference 2025

Topic: Vital Signs in Sports & Health Services with AI

Semi-technical knowledge-sharing on applying AI to analyze biosignals and neural signals for advanced sports and health services. The session covered real-world perspectives from service providers and researchers, highlighting current practice and future opportunities.

Conference Highlights

  • AI for predicting dengue severity in clinical settings
  • Neurofeedback for cognitive performance
  • Brain-Computer Interface for stroke rehabilitation
  • AI and vital signs for athletic performance monitoring
  • AI for Parkinson’s disease screening
  • AI for mental health and stress assessment

Panel: Vital Signs in Sports and Health Services with AI — featuring Patchara Al-umaree alongside industry and research voices, discussing practical AI applications on biosignals and neural signals and sharing notable case studies.

Case highlights included AI-driven biosignal analysis for athletes and neural-signal use cases, underscoring how data-driven methods elevate health and sports performance services.

About NAC 2025

NSTDA Conference #20 — theme: “AI-driven Science and Technology for Sustainable Thailand.” Sessions focused on leveraging AI across healthcare, neuroscience, sports performance, and public health.
